About

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) is a student lead community backed by Google Developers aimed at empowering undergraduate students from all disciplines to grow their knowledge in technology, build solutions for their local communities, and connect with other members from the Google community.

Creating impact and empowering students through technology

Whether you are new to technology and development or you've developing for quite a while, DSC is a place where you can learn technology, try out new ideas, and collaborate to solve real-time problems. In addition to solve great problems, DSC will allow you to connect with other technology enthusiasts from other DSC chapters and the Google Developer Community. We will be hosting events and activities for all students throughout the academic year.
